Your dog won't want to leave after a day at the farm!

We are a family-owned 7 acre farm in Maple Valley, WA. Cedar River Dog farm offers day care and boarding for your furry friend. Your dog will enjoy quality, loving care and play ALL DAY off-leash with their best friends. We live on the farm, so your dog is supervised at all times. Come visit us and see what a special place we offer for dogs.

Cedar River Dog Farm
is a privately owned and operated business offering 14 overnight kennels in our Barn.Each kennel has it's own above-the-ground bed for added comfort. Dogs spend the day in one of our three yards and sleep in their own kennel at night.

Dogs are always supervised during the day. At night we're just feet away if they need us.
