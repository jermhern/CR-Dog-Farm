Cedar River Dog farm is open from February to December, with on-site staff 24/7. Our avaiability in January varies as our staff members take vacation during that time. Please check our FaceBook page for days open in January.

We are open for pick ups and drop offs from 7 am to 6 pm.

Tours, day care, and boarding are by appointment only.

Boarding

Why not give your dog a vacation when you're out of town? Boarding offers your dog an ideal doggie holiday - lots of attention, playing all day with friends, and lounging on the couch!

7 am - 6 pm
