All new dogs must come with their owner for a temperament test/tour. The tour is about 30 minutes to an hour - just depends how long we need to watch your dogs interactions with other dogs.

We only accept dogs that are:

  • 6 months or older
  • Neutered/spayed
  • Fully vaccinated
  • Friendly
  • Not a barker

All medical conditions must be told to us: epilepsy, heart murmur, blind, deaf, etc, so we can take make sure staff knows how to handle any situation that may arise.

We do not offer space for dogs that are dog or people aggressive.

We do not take dogs who have stitches, have seizures more than once every three months, are currently sick, have parasites, or flees.
